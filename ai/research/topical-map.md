# Topical Map — dairy-insurance.com

**Niche:** commercial insurance for **dairy farms / dairy operations** — the herd,
the parlor, the milk, the manure exposure. Distinct from generic farm-owner
coverage and from crop/agriculture broad-line carriers.

## Pillar topics (3)
1. **Dairy Farm Insurance** (root pillar / homepage) — the full coordinated
   program: every coverage line a working dairy needs.
2. **Protecting the Herd & the Milk** — the livestock-mortality + equipment &
   spoilage story unique to dairy (undervalued stock, bulk-tank/chiller failure,
   milk spoilage, product liability).
3. **The Environmental & Liability Exposure** — manure, runoff, lagoon failure,
   agri-tourism, and the workers-comp profile unique to dairy labor.
